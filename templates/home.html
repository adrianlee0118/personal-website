Adrian Lee

Vancouver, BC

Hi my name's Adrian. I'm a Canadian engineer and software developer, a hockey nut, a world adventurer, a gamer and a beer connoisseur. Among other interests, I'm passionate about my relationships, building things, sports, the environment, social equality and continual learning.

I also like teaching; if I'm asked a question about something I understand well, I tend to enjoy explaining it to other people.

Recently I've become an independent software developer on a variety of web and mobile projects influenced by my passions and by my technical background in civil engineering systems and data science.

Before that, I spent 3 years living abroad in Korea and Japan where I taught English and travelled, after 3 years working in the civil/tech engineering profession in my hometown of Vancouver, BC.





About this site

Here, I'll write about programming, technology and life.

To develop this site I've chosen the Flask framework for Python due to simplicity and convenience. Most of the front end is done with the help of Bootstrap.

I've also used several external sources (thanks to their authors). You can check them out in the readme file.

The deploy is done on PythonAnywhere with Google Domains. Thanks to supporting developers and to open-source software! Some resources are also served from the Bootstrap CDN and Cloudflare's free CDN, thanks to them also.

In the event you'd like to take this website as a basis for yours, you can fork it on Github.
